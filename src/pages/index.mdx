# CoAssembly Design co-op

1. CoAssembly Design co-op is Taiwan's first design studio operating under [cooperative principles](https://ica.coop/en/cooperatives/cooperative-identity), co-founded by [I-hao Liao (Mōr)](https://www.behance.net/onemoooooor), [Tzu-Yun Wei](https://www.instagram.com/bushwasroom/), and [Munus Shih](https://munusshih.com).
2. Our work spans brand identity, creative technology, and experimental publishing. We bring together collaborators across disciplines, pairing cultural research with community-oriented methods to support long-term work rather than one-time exchanges.
3. We have designed and collaborated closely with partners including Taiwan Design Research Institute (TDRI), National Taiwan University, Pratt Institute (New York), the Taiwan Pavilion at the Berlin International Film Festival, Tsao Tzu Tou (草字頭), and Asian Labor Future (Thailand).
4. Since 2022, CoAssembly has led the [SpOnAcT!](https://www.instagram.com/spon.act/) project, our community garden where we curated the 2nd [Processing Community Day Taiwan](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zv0il2nKdEc&list=PLaQjHexP0AoYBnqQLZhqW9q-l-lFe2Sy7), bringing together over 20 artists, creative technologists, and educators across six countries and reaching more than 5,000 online participants.
5. We recently published [Contemporary Workers's Survival Guide (vols. 1 & 2)](https://myship.7-11.com.tw/general/detail/GM2603206062729), an initiative to a series of work on labor issues in design, presented at the Tokyo Art Book Fair and the Bergen Art Book Fair in Norway.

<br />
For portfolio or work inquiries, please contact us at hi@coassembly.work.

# 共同集合設計合作社

1. CoAssembly 共同集合設計合作社由[廖一豪（Mōr）](https://www.behance.net/onemoooooor)、[魏姿芸](https://www.instagram.com/bushwasroom/)與[木那斯](https://munusshih.com)共同創辦，是臺灣首間以[合作社原則](https://ica.coop/en/cooperatives/cooperative-identity)運作的設計工作室。
2. 我們的工作涵蓋品牌識別、創意科技與實驗性出版，透過結合文化研究與社群導向的方法，串連不同領域的合作夥伴。我們致力於支持長期投入的合作關係，而非一次性的交流。
3. 我們的合作對象涵蓋臺灣設計研究院（TDRI）、國立臺灣大學、紐約普拉特藝術大學、臺灣柏林影展、草字頭，以及泰國勞動研究組織 Asian Labor Future。
4. 自 2022 年起，CoAssembly 推動 [SpOnAcT! 偶發集合](https://www.instagram.com/spon.act/) 計畫，作為我們的社群花園，並於 2023 年策劃第二屆 [Processing 臺灣國際社群日](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zv0il2nKdEc&list=PLaQjHexP0AoYBnqQLZhqW9q-l-lFe2Sy7)，串聯六個國家、超過二十位藝術家、創意工程師與教育者，並吸引逾五千名線上參與者。
5. 我們近期出版了[《當代勞動者的求生手冊》第一、二冊](https://myship.7-11.com.tw/general/detail/GM2603206062729)，作為設計領域中關注勞動議題的系列工作之始，並於東京藝術書展及挪威卑爾根藝術書展中展出。

如有作品集或合作洽詢，歡迎來信 hi@coassembly.work。
