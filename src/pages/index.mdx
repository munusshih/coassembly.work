# CoAssembly Design co-op

1. CoAssembly is Taiwan's first design studio founded on cooperative (co-op) principles, established by [I-hao Liao (Mōr)](https://www.behance.net/onemoooooor), [Tzu-Yun Wei](https://www.instagram.com/bushwasroom/), and [Munus Shih](https://munusshih.com).
2. Our work spans brand identity design, creative technology, cultural research, and community-based education. We see design as part of a long-term commitment to relationships, and believe meaningful practice emerges through deeper connections with society.
3. We collaborate closely with local and global teams and organizations, including Taiwan Design Research Institute (TDRI), National Taiwan University, Pratt Institute (New York), the Taiwan Pavilion at the Berlin International Film Festival, and Asian Labor Future (Thailand).
4. Since 2022, CoAssembly has led the [SpOnAcT!](https://www.instagram.com/spon.act/) project, curating the 2nd International Processing Community Day in 2023 — connecting over 20 artists, creative engineers, and educators across six countries and reaching more than 5,000 online participants.
5. We recently published Survival Handbook for Contemporary Workers (vols. 1 & 2), presented at the Tokyo Art Book Fair and the Bergen Art Book Fair in Norway.

<br />
For portfolio or work inquiries, please contact us at hi@coassembly.work.

# 共同集合設計合作社

1. CoAssembly 是臺灣第一間以合作社（co-op）原則創立的設計工作室，由[廖一豪（Mōr）](https://www.behance.net/onemoooooor)、[魏姿芸](https://www.instagram.com/bushwasroom/)與[木那斯](https://munusshih.com)共同創辦。
2. 我們的工作涵蓋品牌識別設計、創意科技、文化研究與社區教育。我們將設計視為一種對關係的長期投入，相信有意義的實踐源自與社會更深層的連結。
3. 我們與在地及國際的團隊與組織緊密合作，包括台灣設計研究院（TDRI）、國立臺灣大學、紐約 Pratt Institute、柏林影展台灣館，以及 Asian Labor Future（泰國）。
4. 自 2022 年起，CoAssembly 推動 [SpOnAcT! 偶發集合](https://www.instagram.com/spon.act/) 計畫，並於 2023 年策劃第二屆 Processing 臺灣國際社群日，串聯六個國家、超過二十位藝術家、創意工程師與教育者，並吸引五千多名線上參與者。
5. 我們近期出版了《當代勞動者的求生手冊》第一、二冊，並於東京藝術書展及挪威卑爾根藝術書展中展出。

如有作品集或合作洽詢，歡迎來信 hi@coassembly.work。
